// The Problem

Your ATS is Expensive, Powerful, and Dumb

After 10+ years in technical recruiting at Meta, Citrix, and Cisco, I've used every major ATS on the market. They all share the same fundamental problem: they're databases pretending to be intelligence.

Your ATS holds everything ‚Äî every candidate, every interaction, every note. But when you need to find the right person for a role, you're back to keyword searches, Boolean strings, and manual scrolling through hundreds of profiles hoping something jumps out.

The insight: The ATS isn't the problem. The problem is that it can't reason. What if we gave it a brain?

// The Solution

MCP: A USB Port for AI

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is Anthropic's open standard that lets AI models interact with external systems through well-defined tools. Think of it like a USB port ‚Äî any AI that speaks MCP can plug into any MCP server and use its capabilities.

PAINT is an MCP server purpose-built for recruiting. It connects to your ATS through Merge.dev's unified API, enriches every record with AI-computed insights, and exposes 9 specialized tools that an AI agent can call to search, match, analyze, and audit your recruiting data in real time.

The result: When you talk to Claude (or any MCP-compatible AI), it has direct access to your entire candidate database ‚Äî not as a dumb search, but as an intelligent partner that can reason over your data, remember what you tell it, and make recommendations grounded in reality.

// The Nine Tools

What PAINT Can Do

search_candidates

Search by skills, location, salary, experience. Results are PII-masked. Supports any combination of filters.

get_candidate

Full profile with PAINT enrichments: confidence score, extracted skills, salary expectations, work auth, data issues.

get_candidate_notes

Complete interaction history ‚Äî calls, emails, recruiter observations. This is where the real intelligence lives.

search_jobs

Search open job orders by skills, location, employment type. Returns PAINT-enriched data.

get_job

Full job details including requirements, comp range, remote policy, hiring timeline.

match_candidate_job

The DCR engine. Deep LLM analysis of a candidate-job pair with full reasoning and strategy.

find_matches_for_job

Bulk matching. Ranks all active candidates against a job. Finds the best people across your entire database.

get_pipeline

Submission and pipeline status. Which candidates are where, how long they've been there, what's stuck.

audit_data_health

The Context Health Engine. Grade, dollar cost, action plan. The sales wedge for agencies.
